Research

Multi-Step Sign Language Translation Using Large Language Models

Unpublished

Muhammad Kassim Balogun

This paper explores multi-step Sign Language Translation (SLT) using Large Language Models (LLMs) and Formal SignWriting (FSW), a system of writing sign languages for the intermediate step between the written form of both languages. We use LLMs with decoder-only architecture to learn the mappings between FSW notation and English, utilizing Supervised Fine-Tuning (SFT) with a combination of Contrastive Policy Optimization (CPO) and Simple Preference Optimization (SimPO) on the SignBank+ dataset.
